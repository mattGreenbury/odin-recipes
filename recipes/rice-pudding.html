Rice Pudding

So good your grandma won't be able to stop talking about it!

A simple, old-fashioned rice pudding that can be enjoyed hot or cold.

Smooth textured with a sweet vanilla aroma. Nothing more, nothing less.

Ingredients

  • Whole Milk to cook rice in
  • Granulated Sugar to taste
  • Salt to taste
  • Long-Grain White Rice
  • Vanilla Extract to taste
  • Ground Cinnamon (Optional)

Method

  1. In a large pot, combine milk, granulated sugar, and salt. Bring to a gentle boil over medium heat while stirring.
  2. Once boiling add the washed rice and stir well before reducing heat to a simmer.
  3. Simmer for 50-60 minutes, stirring occasionally until mixture thickens and rice is tender
  4. Remove pot from heat and stir in vanilla.
  5. Once cooled slightly stir in extra milk till desired consistency is achieved.
  6. Serve hot or cold, with or without cinnamon and or extra milk.
